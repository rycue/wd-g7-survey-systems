Introduction

The Disaster Preparedness Survey System is designed to collect important information from individuals and households regarding their level of readiness for natural and human-made disasters. The data gathered will help communities, researchers, and organizations better understand current preparedness levels and identify areas for improvement.

The primary purpose of this survey is to support academic research by assessing awareness, resources, and preparedness actions related to disasters such as typhoons, floods, earthquakes, and other emergencies. The results will be used to develop recommendations and contribute to disaster risk reduction and management studies.

Privacy Notice

Your privacy is important to us. All information collected in this survey will be used strictly for school research purposes only. The data gathered will be handled responsibly and will not be used in any way that violates applicable laws and regulations. Personal information will be kept confidential and will only be accessed by the researchers involved in this study. Any results or reports will present data in a summarized form and will not include personally identifiable information. Participation in this survey is voluntary, and you may choose not to answer any question or withdraw at any time.
